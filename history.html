“天數有變，神器更易，而歸有德之人。”

I spare my free time reading literatures about Chinese history tracing back 3700 years. Annalistic literatures, for instance Zizhi Tongjian, impress me clearly in time order about the rise and fall of Chinese dynasties, while biographical literatures, for instance Book of Northern Qi and History of Ming, tell me the detailed progress of individuals and events.
